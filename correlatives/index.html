    Correlatives¶

    Table of Correlatives¶

    Format:

    • singular/plural OR any
    • example

    Relative, interrogative, and demonstrative¶

    What (Relative and interrogative) That (Demonstrative)
    Person/individual -u qua/qui
    who    		 (i)ta/(i)ti
    that (one)/those (ones)
    Thing -o quo
    what    		 (i)to
    that (thing)
    Plural -i
    Adjective -a
    Motive pro
    Possession di
    Place -loke, -u ube
    where    		 ibe
    there
    Time -tempe kande
    when    		 lore
    then
    Way/Manner -e, -maniere quale
    how    		 tale
    that way
    Quality -a, -speca quala
    what kind of    		 tala
    such (of that kind), such like
    Quantity -e? quante
    how much    		 tante
    that much
    Quantity – noun -o quanto
    quantity, amount    		 tanto
    that much
    Quantity – adjective -a quanta
    how much, how many    		 tanta
    that much

    Indefinite quantities¶

    Some (Indeterminate) Any (Most indeterminate) Other
    Person ulu/uli
    someone    		 irgu/irgi
    anyone    		 altru/altri
    someone else
    Thing ulo
    something    		 irgo
    anything    		 altro
    something else
    Place ulube
    somewhere    		 irgube
    anywhere    		 altrube
    elsewhere
    Time ulatempe
    sometime    		 irgatempe
    anytime    		 altratempe
    at another time
    Way ulamaniere
    somehow    		 irgamaniere
    in any way    		 altramaniere
    in another way
    Quality ulaspeca
    some kind of    		 irgaspeca
    any kind of    		 altraspeca
    another kind of
    Quantity kelke
    some    		 irga quanto
    however much    		 altra quanto
    another quantity

    Definite quantities¶

    No / none (Negative) Every (Collective)
    Person nulu
    no one    		 omnu/omni
    everyone
    Thing nulo
    nothing    		 omno
    everything
    Place nulube
    nowhere    		 omnube
    everywhere
    Time nulatempe
    never    		 sempre
    always
    Way nulamaniere
    in no way    		 omnamaniere
    in every way
    Quality nulaspeca
    no kind of    		 omnaspeca
    all kinds of
    Quantity nulo
    none    		 omno
    all

    1. The initial syllable can omitted: ta, to, ti, ta, pro to, di to. ↩

    2. Because of the ease of pronunciation, the first word ending can be omitted: ultempe, nultempe, ulspeca, nulspeca, ulmaniere, nulmaniere. ↩

    3. omnatempe is technically correct and in turn usable, but sempre is the official word. ↩
